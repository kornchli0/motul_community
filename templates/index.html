Community Rules

  • We Value Time: Every route is designed to fit busy schedules and bring maximum enjoyment.
  • Safety and Support: We ride with respect for the rules and prioritize safety.
  • This is Not a Business: Contributions are used solely for organizing events and supporting the community.
  • Freedom to Participate: Choose your style: short rides or long challenges.
  • Riding for the Soul: We share a passion for motorcycles and spending time with friends.
